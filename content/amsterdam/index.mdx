perfectionism?) of "wasting" analog film material. So one evening I loaded a
fresh TMAX400 and cycled out to take pictures of whatever captured my attention
(and not return until the film is full).

Below a selection (of mostly smombies) on a regular Thursday evening in
Amsterdam.
