While visiting my godfather in Amsterdam, I resolved to confront my reservations or perhaps perfectionism about 'wasting' analog film material. Thus, one evening, I loaded a fresh TMAX400 into my camera and embarked on a cycling journey, capturing whatever intrigued me along the way, steadfast in my resolve not to return until the film reel was full.

Below a selection (of mostly smombies) on a regular Thursday evening in Amsterdam.
